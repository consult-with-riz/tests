§04 Process

How we work — from intake to first post in under two weeks.

STEP 01

Voice intake

A 90-minute conversation. We map how you think, what you've earned the right to say, and the people you want to reach.

STEP 02

Editorial calendar

You approve a four-week calendar of post angles. We draft. You review. Two rounds of revision before we ship.

STEP 03

Publish + engage

We post under your name. We engage with the right people daily. You stay in the loop without being in the workflow.

STEP 04

Review + iterate

Monthly performance review. Voice tightens. The library of what works grows. The brand compounds.

§05 Frequently Asked

Questions worth answering before you book a call.

Will the content actually sound like me?
Yes. Voice intake takes 90 minutes and produces a written voice document we work from for the rest of the engagement. You see drafts before anything ships. Most clients say their first post sounds more like them than what they were writing themselves.
Who's writing the content — AI or humans?
Humans. Specifically, editors with B2B operator backgrounds. We use AI as a research and structuring tool. We don't ship AI-generated drafts.
How long until I see results?
Visibility shifts in 4–6 weeks. Inbound shifts in 8–12. We don't promise viral hits. We promise compounding presence.
Do you do LinkedIn ads or Sales Navigator outreach?
Not as a core service. We focus on organic presence and engagement. If you want paid distribution, we can scope that as a separate project.
What's the contract length?
Three-month minimum, then month-to-month. Personal branding doesn't work in 30-day sprints.
Who's not a good fit?
Pre-seed founders, agencies, coaches, anyone who wants viral growth tactics, anyone who wants to be hands-off the content entirely. We need 60 minutes of your input per month for this to work.
